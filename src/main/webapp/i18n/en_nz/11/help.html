RUBY 7s

Let the radiant, resplendent reds of Ruby 7s bring you riches! Win prizes by revealing Ruby 7 symbols. Reveal a double Ruby 7 symbol to double the prize and triple Ruby 7 symbol to triple the prize! When 3 or more special Bonus Ruby symbols are revealed, the bonus game is triggered, giving you a chance to spin the wheel and win even more glittering prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Ruby symbol to reveal Ruby 7 symbols, Gem symbols, or Bonus Ruby symbols. Reveal any Ruby 7 symbol and win a prize. Find a double Ruby 7 symbol and double the prize, and a triple Ruby 7 symbol to triple the prize.

Reveal 3 or more Bonus Ruby symbols to trigger the bonus game. Press the circular arrow to spin the wheel for a chance to win more prizes. Win a prize when the pointer lands on a prize value. Prizes accumulate until the bonus ends. Advance to the next wheel with richer prizes when the pointer lands on an Arrow symbol. Landing on a red X removes one Bonus Ruby. The bonus game ends when no more Bonus Rubies remain.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
